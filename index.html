My Projects

Diagnose of Eye-Related Disease Using Deep Learning

An AI-powered system to detect eye diseases like diabetic retinopathy and glaucoma from retinal images. Helps doctors with faster and more accurate diagnosis.

Built using deep learning to analyze retinal images and identify diseases early. Uses a dataset from Kaggle, trained with CNN for high accuracy. Allows easy image upload and instant disease prediction with a confidence score.

IoT Based Water Purifier

A smart water purifier that monitors water quality in real-time and sends alerts when purification is required.

Uses IoT sensors to check water quality parameters like TDS and pH. Sends notifications through a mobile app for timely maintenance. Ensures safe and clean drinking water with minimal manual checks.

Alcohol Detector in Vehicle

A vehicle safety system that prevents the engine from starting if alcohol is detected in the driver’s breath.

Uses a breath alcohol sensor to detect alcohol levels. If detected above the limit, the system automatically stops the vehicle from starting. Helps reduce accidents caused by drunk driving.
